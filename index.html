Econo Lodge Inn & Suites®

Houston NW-Cy-Fair

The Econo Lodge Inn & Suites® Houston NW-CY-Fair hotel is easy to find, easy to book and easy on your wallet. Conveniently located near Willowbrook Mall and University of Houston Downtown, our discount hotel puts you near plenty of local sites, activities and attractions to explore and enjoy. Whether in you’re town for work or vacation, we offer the basics for travelers to have a relaxing stay. Need recommendations for dinner? Our friendly inviting staff will be on standby to answer questions or give directions.

Traveling for work? Guests in town for business will appreciate access to our copy and fax machines. Stay in touch with family or coworkers online when you connect to our free premium WiFi, available throughout the hotel. Our Econo Lodge hotel cares about the environment and has taken active steps to support green hotel practices.

We offer an experience that simplifies travel, along with amenities that will help you stick to your budget. Featuring flat-screen TVs, and warm, premium bedding with plenty of pillows, you’ll feel right at home in one of our guest rooms. Have leftovers from dinner or snacks? You’ll also find an in-room microwave and refrigerator. Enjoy conveniently placed outlets in your night stand and charge all your digital gadgets. After a long day of work or exploring the area, come back to the hotel and unwind with a cup free hot coffee.
